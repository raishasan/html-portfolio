Movie Ranking

Top 3 picks of all time

Margin Call

When an analyst uncovers information that could ruin them all, the key players (Kevin Spacey, Paul Bettany) at an investment firm take extreme measures to control the damage.

One Piece

Monkey D. Luffy sets off on an adventure with his pirate crew in hopes of finding the greatest treasure ever, known as the "One Piece."

Jab Tak Hai Jaan

A bomb disposal expert becomes bitter and lonely and is unable to fall in love until he is forced to deal with his past.
